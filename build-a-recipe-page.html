Brazilian Strogonoff

Strogonoff is a popular Brazilian comfort dish made with tender pieces of chicken or beef cooked in a creamy tomato-based sauce. It is quick to prepare, flavorful, and usually served with white rice and crispy potato sticks. This recipe is perfect for a simple homemade meal that everyone can enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast (diced)
  • Onion (chopped)
  • Oil or butter
  • Heavy cream
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Mustard and Mushrooms (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil or butter in a pan over medium heat and saut√© the onion and garlic until soft.
  2. Add the diced chicken and cook until it is golden and fully cooked.
  3. Stir in the tomato sauce (or ketchup), mustard, salt, and black pepper. Let it simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Add the heavy cream and mix well until the sauce becomes creamy and smooth.
  5. Turn off the heat and serve hot with white rice and potato sticks.
